Turbo Highway - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Turbo Highway ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Turbo Highway does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress (levels, coins, best scores) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to serve relevant ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play.

4. Data Storage

All game data (scores, progress, unlocked items) is stored locally on your device using device storage. This data is not transmitted to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

Turbo Highway is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays ads appropriate for general audiences.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Billing - For processing in-app purchases

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
