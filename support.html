Common questions

Levels 41–60 are locked. How do I open them?

The game gates each phase behind stars, not level count. You need 35 stars to open Phase 2 (levels 21–40), and 70 stars to open Phase 3 (levels 41–60).

Every level awards up to 3 stars based on how far you drive. If you're stuck, replay earlier levels you finished with 1 or 2 stars — those are the cheapest stars to collect.

I lost my coins and progress

Progress is stored on your device, not in an account. Deleting the app deletes your save, and there's no cloud backup yet.

If your progress vanished without you deleting anything, email me with your device model — that's a bug and I want to see it.

How do I remove the ads?

There's no paid ad-removal option right now. Ads appear between runs and are how the game pays for itself.

The rewarded ad — the one that revives you after a crash — is always optional. Skip it and you'll go straight to the results screen.

The controls feel unresponsive

Your car steers by dragging anywhere on the road, not by tapping the car itself. Keep your thumb down and move — don't lift between lanes.

If the whole game stutters, close background apps and restart it. If that doesn't fix it, email me your device model.

Can I play without an internet connection?

Yes. Every level, the Garage, and Free Drive work fully offline. Only the ads need a connection — without one, they're skipped.

What's a near-miss combo?

Squeeze past a traffic car without hitting it and you bank a near miss. Chain them and the multiplier climbs: x2 at 5, x3 at 10, x5 at 20.

Every coin you pick up is multiplied by your current combo. Crashing resets it to zero — nothing else does.

Power-ups

  • HeartRestores one life. You start every run with three.
  • ShieldAbsorbs one crash without costing a heart.
  • MagnetPulls nearby coins to you for 5 seconds.
  • Double coinsDoubles every coin for 6 seconds — stacks on top of your combo.
  • NitroA hard speed burst. More distance, less reaction time.
  • Slow motionDrops the world speed so you can thread heavy traffic.
  • GhostDrive straight through traffic, briefly. The rarest drop in the game.
